Description

The Emergency Preparedness merit badge teaches Scouts essential skills for preparing for and responding to emergencies. This merit badge helps Scouts develop the knowledge and abilities needed to handle various emergency situations, from natural disasters to personal emergencies.

Requirements

  1. Emergency Planning
    • Learn emergency planning basics
    • Create a family emergency plan
    • Understand emergency contacts
    • Know evacuation procedures
  2. Emergency Kits
    • Learn essential supplies
    • Create a home emergency kit
    • Prepare a portable kit
    • Know kit maintenance
  3. Emergency Communications
    • Learn communication methods
    • Practice emergency signals
    • Understand radio use
    • Know alert systems
  4. Natural Disasters
    • Learn about common disasters
    • Practice safety procedures
    • Understand warning signs
    • Know response actions
  5. First Aid and Safety
    • Learn emergency first aid
    • Practice safety measures
    • Understand triage
    • Know rescue basics
  6. Community Service
    • Learn about emergency services
    • Practice community involvement
    • Understand volunteer roles
    • Know how to help

Pro Tip: For requirement 2, create comprehensive emergency kits for different scenarios. Include items for both home and portable use. Practice using each item in your kits and regularly check expiration dates. Remember to customize kits based on your family's specific needs.
