Description

The First Aid merit badge teaches Scouts essential emergency care and life-saving skills. This merit badge helps Scouts develop the knowledge and abilities needed to respond to medical emergencies, potentially saving lives in critical situations.

Requirements

  1. Emergency Response
    • Learn emergency action steps
    • Practice scene assessment
    • Understand emergency priorities
    • Know when to call for help
  2. Basic First Aid
    • Learn wound care
    • Practice bandaging techniques
    • Understand infection prevention
    • Know how to handle bleeding
  3. CPR and AED
    • Learn CPR techniques
    • Practice rescue breathing
    • Understand AED use
    • Know when to use each
  4. Injury Management
    • Learn to handle fractures
    • Practice splinting techniques
    • Understand head injuries
    • Know spinal injury care
  5. Medical Conditions
    • Learn to recognize symptoms
    • Practice treatment methods
    • Understand common conditions
    • Know emergency signs
  6. First Aid Kit
    • Learn essential supplies
    • Practice kit organization
    • Understand supply use
    • Know maintenance needs

Pro Tip: For requirement 1, practice emergency scenarios regularly with qualified instructors. Focus on staying calm, assessing situations quickly, and following proper procedures. Remember that your safety is always the top priority in any emergency.
