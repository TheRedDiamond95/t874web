Description

The Swimming merit badge teaches Scouts essential swimming skills and water safety. This merit badge helps Scouts develop the knowledge and abilities needed to swim safely and confidently in various water environments.

Requirements

  1. Swimming Basics
    • Learn swimming strokes
    • Practice water entry
    • Understand breathing techniques
    • Know safety rules
  2. Water Safety
    • Learn safety procedures
    • Practice rescue techniques
    • Understand water hazards
    • Know emergency response
  3. Swimming Skills
    • Learn different strokes
    • Practice endurance swimming
    • Understand diving basics
    • Know water games
  4. Rescue Techniques
    • Learn rescue methods
    • Practice reaching assists
    • Understand throwing assists
    • Know self-rescue
  5. Water Activities
    • Learn water sports
    • Practice snorkeling
    • Understand boating safety
    • Know water games
  6. Physical Fitness
    • Learn conditioning
    • Practice endurance
    • Understand nutrition
    • Know rest periods

Pro Tip: For requirement 3, focus on mastering the basic strokes first (freestyle, breaststroke, backstroke) before moving on to more advanced techniques. Practice regularly in a safe environment with proper supervision. Remember to build endurance gradually and always follow safety guidelines.
