Eagle Required Merit Badges

Eagle Scout Requirements Last updated: March 15, 2024

To earn the rank of Eagle Scout, a Scout must earn a total of 21 merit badges, including all of the following required merit badges. Click on any merit badge to learn more about its requirements and resources.

Citizenship

  • Citizenship in the Nation
  • Citizenship in the World
  • Citizenship in the Community

Outdoor Skills

  • Camping
  • Cooking
  • First Aid

Personal Development

  • Personal Fitness
  • Personal Management
  • Swimming

Emergency Preparedness

  • Emergency Preparedness
  • Lifesaving

Environmental Science

  • Environmental Science
  • Sustainability

Communication

  • Communication
  • Family Life

Note: In addition to these required merit badges, Scouts must earn a total of 21 merit badges to achieve the rank of Eagle Scout. The remaining merit badges can be chosen from any of the available merit badges offered by the Boy Scouts of America.
